Where it started 

            December 7th, 2025
            By Christopher Kollar

            In strange voyeurism,
            my window in the night
            shines, blue light and flickers
            your smile, pretty and far.

            And I hate you so,
            As rain hates the waves
            it returns to forever,
            again and again.

            Hate bites down on me
            like a night sky
            always ending right where
            it started.
